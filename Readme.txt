Adding a high conversion Stripe Checkout form to your site just got easier with Simple Stripe Checkout Companion.

== Description ==

The plugin is an add-on for Stripe Checkout which allows you to insert pre-written shortcode examples directly into your post and pages.

Instead of spending time messing with your own shortcodes, you can simply insert and customize ours to meet your needs.

> "Stripe Checkout is an embeddable payment form for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. It works within your siteâ€”customers can pay instantly, without being redirected away to complete the transaction."

###Plugin Requirements###

This plugin requires the Simple Stripe Checkout plugin.[Get Simple Stripe Checkout](https://wordpress.org/plugins/stripe/)


###Available in Stripe Checkout Companion Pro Only###

There is also a Pro version of Companion that works with Stripe Checkout Pro and contains the following shortcode support.

* **User Entered Amounts** - Allow customers enter what they want to pay.
* **Coupon Codes** - Setup discount codes in your Stripe dashboard for customers to apply to their total.
* **Custom Fields** - Record additional data along with each Stripe payment.
* **Subscriptions** - Let customers pay and sign up for your Stripe recurring plans.

== Installation ==

There are three ways to install this plugin.

= 1. Admin Search =
1. NOTE: Make sure that you have the Simple Checkout Pro installed.
1. In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add.
1. Search for `Stripe Checkout Companion`.
1. Find the plugin that's labeled `Simple Stripe Checkout Companion`.
1. Look for the author name `Kyle Brown` on the plugin.
1. Click to install.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. A new submenu item `Companion` will appear beneath the Stripe Checkout menu.

= 2. Download & Upload =
1. Download the plugin (a zip file) on the right column of this page.
1. In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add.
1. Select the tab "Upload".
1. Upload the .zip file you just downloaded.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. A new submenu item `Companion` will appear beneath the Stripe Checkout menu.

= 3. FTP Upload =
1. Download the plugin (.zip file) on the right column of this page.
1. Unzip the zip file contents.
1. Upload the `simple stripe checkout companion` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory of your site.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. A new submenu item `Companion` will appear beneath the Stripe Checkout menu.
